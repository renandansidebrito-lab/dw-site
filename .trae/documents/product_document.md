## Project Overview
Professional website for a marble and granite company that operates in three main sectors: Serraria (Cutting), Chapas (Slabs), and Recortado (Cut-to-size).

## Business Requirements

### Company Profile
- **Industry**: Marble and Granite Processing
- **Business Sectors**: 
  1. Serraria (Stone Cutting Services)
  2. Chapas (Slab Sales)
  3. Recortado (Custom Cut-to-size Services)
- **Target Audience**: Construction companies, architects, interior designers, and end consumers

### Website Objectives
1. Showcase company capabilities across all three business sectors
2. Display product portfolio and services
3. Provide contact information and facilitate customer inquiries
4. Establish professional online presence
5. Support business growth through digital marketing

## Technical Requirements

### Frontend Technology Stack
- **Framework**: React with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for responsive design
- **State Management**: Zustand for application state
- **Routing**: React Router DOM for navigation
- **Icons**: Lucide React for consistent iconography

### Website Structure
1. **Homepage**: Company overview and sector highlights
2. **Serraria Page**: Cutting services, equipment showcase, capabilities
3. **Chapas Page**: Slab catalog, materials, specifications
4. **Recortado Page**: Custom cutting services, precision work examples
5. **Contact Page**: Company information, contact forms, location

### Design Requirements
- **Style**: Modern, professional, clean design
- **Color Scheme**: Neutral tones reflecting stone materials (grays, whites, earth tones)
- **Typography**: Professional, readable fonts
- **Imagery**: High-quality stone material photos
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design approach

### Content Requirements
- Company history and background
- Detailed service descriptions for each sector
- Product galleries with stone varieties
- Technical specifications and capabilities
- Contact information and business hours
- Location and facilities information
